Heat of Hydration of Cement

Objective:

To determine the heat of hydration of a given sample of cement.

Material used:

Calorimeter, mortar and pestle, stopwatch or timer, muffle furnace, analytical balance, weighing bottles, camel hair brush, dessicator, nitric acid (2N strength), hydrofluoric acid (40% w/w), zinc oxide, paraffin wax, distilled water.
